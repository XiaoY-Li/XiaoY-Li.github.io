I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science at the Nanjing University, under the supervising of Dr. Jia Liu. Previously, I received my B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). My primary research interests focus on AI-driven mobile computing, including wireless localization and intelligent sensing.
